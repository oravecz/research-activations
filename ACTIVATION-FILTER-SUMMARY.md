## Overview
Filtered database to keep only **true brand activations** - special promotional events designed to drive traffic beyond ordinary foot traffic. Removed all permanent store openings and standard retail operations.

## Definition of Brand Activation
A brand activation is a **special promotional event or program** within a retail environment that:
- Is time-limited or rotating (pop-ups, temporary experiences, event series)
- Creates special experiences beyond everyday shopping
- Drives incremental foot traffic through unique programming
- Involves partnerships, events, workshops, or interactive experiences

**NOT included:** Permanent store openings, flagship launches, standard retail operations, or everyday store features.
